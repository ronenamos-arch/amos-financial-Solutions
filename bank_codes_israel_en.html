What is a Bank Code?

The Bank of Israel assigns numbers to banks in Israel, so each bank in Israel has a unique bank code (bank number). The code is used for various financial purposes:

  • Check Details Update - Accounting management requires recording the bank code
  • Bank Account Debit - Requires bank number, branch number and account number
  • Bank Transfers - For identifying the receiving bank
  • Accounting Management - For recording and tracking different bank accounts
